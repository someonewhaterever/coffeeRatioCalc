Coffee Tips

Use Fresh Beans

Freshly roasted beans make a huge difference. Always check the roast date on the bag.

Grind Just Before Brewing

Grinding beans right before brewing keeps the flavors locked in and vibrant.

Control Your Water Temp

Ideal temperature is 90–96°C. Too hot burns coffee, too cold under-extracts.

Use Filtered Water

Tap water with too many minerals can ruin the taste. Use clean, filtered water.

Keep Your Gear Clean

Regularly clean your coffee maker and grinder to avoid bitter buildup.
